Ghana Senior High Schools

This website provides detailed information on senior high schools in Ghana. Since such data is not easily accessible online, the site helps students make informed choices about their schools based on their personal preferences and interests.

Novari Study

I believe that studying should be fun and engaging, not just a chore. That's why I created Novari Study, a study app that uses AI to help you learn and retain information more effectively. With Novari Study, you can create personalized study plans, track your progress, and get instant feedback on your answers. You can also access a vast library of study tools, including flashcards, quizzes, and practice tests. Whether you're a student, a teacher, or just someone who wants to learn something new, Novari Study is designed to make learning more enjoyable and productive. While the app is still in development, most features are already functional and available for testing.
